College History

The foundations of this premier educational institution were laid in the year 1992 under the progressive vision of the Yuva Gramin Vikas Sanstha, a parent trust established specifically to counter academic backwardness and drive socio-economic development in rural regions. Spearheaded by the visionary leadership of Founder President Shri. Prakashrao A. Abitkar, who serves as the Health Minister of Maharashtra and Guardian Minister of Kolhapur District, the institute was created to bridge the steep urban-rural divide by providing industrial-grade technical exposure to regional student populations. The entire engineering complex is beautifully situated within the sprawling, eco-friendly 100-acre Abitkar Knowledge City (AKC) campus in the scenic Bhudargad Taluka, offering students a highly peaceful, healthy, and disciplined learning environment. Officially established as a modern engineering campus in 2024, the college operates as a Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) recognised and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) approved un-aided facility. To ensure the highest standard of academic compliance, the institute is officially affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU), Lonere, which awards the official degrees upon graduation. Under the current guidance of President Shri. Arjun Anandrao Abitkar and Principal Dr. Amar R. Chougule, the college offers five major four-year Undergraduate (B.Tech) branches, including Computer Engineering with 120 seats, and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering, each hosting 60 seats. To expand its educational reach, the campus also hosts three distinct Polytechnic Diploma tracks focusing on Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. The institution is highly regarded for its experienced faculty, state-of-the-art laboratory spaces, high-speed computer centers, a massive centralized research library, and an active campus culture that blends technical seminars with regional sports tournaments and grand annual prize distribution festivals. By combining rigid university curriculums with corporate interview training modules, the college successfully transforms rural youth into globally competitive, ethically responsible engineering professionals and researchers.

Institutional Vision

  • To sustain a premier learning hub that empowers rural youth through highly disciplined, top-tier technical education.
  • To transform young talent into industry-ready engineers and competent researchers capable of matching global socio-economic shifts.

Institutional Mission

  • Educational Excellence: Providing quality technical skills for nation-building.
  • Skill Development: Supplying foundational engineering knowledge to students and staff.
  • Research and Collaboration: Fostering an environment for industrial consulting and research.
  • Industry Alignment: Meeting the evolving needs of society and the corporate sector.
  • Value Inculcation: Promoting social and ethical values.
