e-RaktKosh is an online platform that connects all blood banks across India into a single digital network. Managed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, this government initiative allows citizens and hospitals to check live blood component availability by state, district, and blood group in real time. It simplifies the donation process by enabling voluntary donors to register online, schedule appointments, and download digital donor cards. Furthermore, the system enforces strict quality standards by tracking professional screening, shelf-life expiration, and proper storage guidelines. By digitizing the entire supply chain, e-RaktKosh enhances transparency and ensures quick access to safe blood during critical medical emergencies.
