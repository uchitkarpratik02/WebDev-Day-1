Fruit

Mango

  • Colour: Yellow / Green
  • Season: Summer

Mango is universally celebrated as the king of fruits due to its rich taste and vibrant tropical aroma. This delicious fruit thrives excellently across various states of India during the warm and sunny months. It is highly valued for its sweet, juicy pulp which is used in making traditional desserts, shakes, and pickles. Culturally, mango leaves and fruits hold a deeply auspicious place in many traditional Indian festivals and celebrations. It remains a popular summer staple that brings refreshing joy and unique flavor profiles to millions of households.

Apple

  • Colour: Red / Green
  • Season: Winter / Autumn

Apple is a highly nutritious and globally favored fruit that grows predominantly in the cooler, hilly northern regions of India. It is famous for its crisp texture, sweet taste, and exceptional health benefits that support overall daily well-being. Regular consumption of this fruit provides essential vitamins, dietary fibers, and antioxidants necessary for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Crisp apples are widely enjoyed fresh as a snack, baked into pies, or processed into pure juices and vinegars. Its remarkable storage capability allows people to enjoy its distinct crunch and nutritional value throughout the year.
